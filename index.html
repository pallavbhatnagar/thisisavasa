Exit Music is the creative identity of Pallav Bhatnagar — a rising voice in the world of music and film scoring. Born and raised in India, Pallav has been immersed in music since his early years, shaping a sound that fuses electronic textures with sweeping cinematic energy.

Blurring the lines between the concert hall and the big screen, Exit Music is less about genres and more about creating worlds. Every composition is designed as an immersive experience — bold, emotional, and charged with narrative weight. His music doesn’t just play in the background; it moves with the story, carrying with it a depth and resonance that lingers.

Driven by a belief that music has the power to inspire, heal, and connect, Exit Music crafts soundscapes that feel both deeply personal and universally cinematic. With relentless dedication and a vision that pulls equally from contemporary culture and Hollywood’s most iconic scores, he’s carving his lane as one of the next generation’s standout composers.
This isn’t just the beginning — Exit Music is building a legacy, one score at a time
