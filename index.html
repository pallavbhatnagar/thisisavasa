Discover Avasa


Avasa is the moniker of Pallav Bhatnagar, A musician who is just starting on his journey, as a composer. Born and raised in India, Avasa has been studying music since his sophomore years and has developed a unique talent for composing pieces that span a wide range of genres from Electronic to Filmic.

With a deep love of music and a passion for storytelling, Avasa is committed in creating eccentric and immersive soundscapes for film and television. His work is characterized by its emotional depth, technical proficiency, and attention to detail aiding his core belief that music has the ability to inspire, heal, and connect people.

With hard work, dedication, and a willingness to learn, Avasa is on the path to becoming a successful and celebrated film composer in impending years.
